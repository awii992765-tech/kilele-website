About Us

Kilele Water is purified drinking water bottled at source and packed by Elmic Limited,
a consumer goods company based in Nairobi. Since we started our operations in 2018,
we have stayed true to our mission of providing clean, safe, and reliable water to our customers
wherever they are whenever they want.
