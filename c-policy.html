Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 17, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. These files allow websites to recognize your device and store information about your preferences and browsing history. Cookies are an essential part of modern web functionality and help improve user experience by remembering settings, login information, and usage patterns.

2. What Cookies Does Kilele Water Use?

Kilele Water uses various types of cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide better services. The cookies we use fall into the following categories:

2.1 Essential/Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and enable core features such as:

  • 1.User authentication and login functionality
  • 2.Session management
  • 3. Security and fraud prevention
  • 4.Payment processing
  • 5.Compliance with legal requirements

Without these cookies, services you have requested cannot be provided. These cookies are enabled by default and cannot be disabled.

2.2 Performance/Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. They allow us to:

  • 1.Track page visits and user behavior
  • 2.Measure website performance and speed
  • 3.Identify technical issues
  • 4.Understand traffic sources
  • 5.Analyze which pages are most popular

This information helps us improve our website and services. We use tools such as Google Analytics for this purpose.

2.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience, such as:

  • 1.Language preferences
  • 2.Font size adjustments
  • 3.Saved form information
  • 4.User preferences and settings
  • 5.Video and media player preferences

2.4 Marketing/Behavioral Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits and interests to deliver targeted advertising and marketing content. They may be set by us or by third-party advertising partners and allow us to:

  • 1.Display relevant advertisements
  • 2.Create marketing campaigns
  • 3.Track campaign effectiveness
  • 4.Re-target interested users
  • 5.Understand user interests and preferences

5. Legal Basis for Cookie Use

We use cookies based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For marketing and non-essential cookies, we obtain your explicit consent through cookie consent banners

  • Legal Obligation: To comply with legal requirements and regulations
  • Contract Performance: To provide services you request

6. How to Manage Your Cookie Preferences

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through your settings.

6.1 Browser Controls

You can control and manage cookies through your web browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data
  • Internet Explorer: Settings → Internet Options → General → Browsing history

6.2 Cookie Consent Manager

You can adjust your cookie preferences directly through our website's cookie consent banner or settings. When you first visit our site, you will see a cookie notice allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies.

6.3 Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. If your browser sends a DNT signal, Kilele Water will respect your preference to limit tracking, although certain essential cookies may still be used for website functionality.

6.4 Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of certain third-party cookies using the following tools:

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI): Visit NAI Opt-out Tool
  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA): Visit DAA Opt-out Tool

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Specifically:

  • 1.You may not be able to log in to your account
  • 2.Some website features may not function properly
  • 3.Your preferences and settings will not be saved
  • 4.You may see less relevant content and advertisements

Essential cookies required for basic website functionality cannot be disabled without losing core features.
