As a company, we are mindful of our customers' health and wellness. And that is why we have put in place high-grade water purification systems that produce pure drinking water with exceptional quality and refreshing taste.

Here at Elmic Limited, we have installed a production capacity of about 6000 liters per day. We, therefore, can confidently guarantee all our customers of consistent and reliable water supply including high demand events like concerts, parties, functions etc.

All our processes and bottled water have been duly certified by the Kenya Bureau of Standards (KEBS). To this end, we have KS EAS 153 for packaged drinking water standards as well as KS 459 part 9: Code of hygiene standards. So, when gulping a bottle of Kilele Water, be rest assured that you are enjoying the best in quality and safety!
